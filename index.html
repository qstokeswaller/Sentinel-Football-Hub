The Problem

SA football runs on WhatsApp groups and outdated spreadsheets.

No single source of truth. Player data is scattered across phones and notebooks, match preparation takes hours, and player development goes unmeasured. We built the fix — for clubs and players alike.

Fragmented Data

Player records split across phones, group chats, and paper registers.

Wasted Prep Time

Coaches spend hours on admin that should take minutes.

No Real Analysis

Players get vague feedback. Real performance data never reaches them.

Invisible Development

Our Purpose

Why Sentinel exists.

We built this because the tools exist. They just weren't accessible to SA football — until now.

Mission

Give every club at every level access to the operational tools previously reserved for professional and semi-professional organisations. Give every player access to the kind of structured analysis that accelerates real development.

Vision

A South African football ecosystem where every grassroots club operates with the same structure and insight as a top-flight operation — and every player, at every level, has access to honest, expert analysis of their game.
