Why We Built This

The vision behind
every module.

SA football is full of dedicated coaches running operations on WhatsApp groups, shared spreadsheets, and sheer force of will. Sentinel exists to change that — by connecting your entire club into one intelligent system that automates the admin, tracks the data, and frees your staff to focus on the thing that actually matters.

One connected operation — end to end

Every module feeds every other. Attendance automatically updates player profiles. Match events generate statistics without any manual re-entry. Scouting records connect to squad management. Your entire club operation is a single, coherent, always-current picture — not a collection of disconnected tools.

Coach football. Not admin.

The hours coaches spend on spreadsheets, message threads, and paper registers make no player better. Sentinel eliminates the busywork — one-tap attendance capture, automated session logs, instant match report templates. The admin manages itself. Your staff spend that reclaimed time where it actually belongs: on the pitch.

Player development tracked precisely — not estimated

Development conversations backed by real data change outcomes. Every session attended, every match assessed, every statistic generated is stored per player — automatically, continuously. Coaches walk into player reviews with objective evidence, not gut feel. Scouts and technical directors have a full development record to evaluate against, always available and always current.

Built for SA football — not adapted for it

We didn't take a generic sports management tool and rebrand it. Sentinel was designed from the ground up for the SA football context — where resources are limited, WhatsApp is the default infrastructure, and exceptional coaching talent has been held back by the tools available to it. The professional operational standard is no longer reserved for the few.

Custom Development

Beyond the platform —
we build for you.

The standard platform covers the needs of most clubs. But if you have a specific workflow, a custom integration requirement, or a feature that simply doesn't exist yet — we take on custom development work for clubs that need something built to their exact specifications. Your platform, extended your way, for a one-off development fee.

Club-Specific Features

Have a workflow or operational tool that's unique to how your club runs? We design and build it directly into your platform — fully integrated, not bolted on. From scoping and design to delivery, we handle the entire process.

System Integrations

Connect Sentinel to your existing infrastructure — GPS tracking devices, biometric systems, federation databases, or external data sources. If it has an API or a structured data format, we can build the bridge into your platform.

Custom Reports & Exports

Need data produced in a specific format for federation reporting, sponsor presentations, or board-level summaries? We build custom report templates and automated export pipelines shaped exactly around what your organisation needs to produce.

Club Branding & White-Label

Full visual alignment with your club's identity — branded dashboards, your colour system, your logo throughout every interface. For academies and professional clubs that need the platform to feel entirely theirs, not a third-party tool.

Multi-Club & Academy Infrastructure

Managing multiple affiliated clubs, age groups, or partner organisations? We build the cross-club management layer your setup requires — shared scouting pipelines, consolidated reporting, and custom access structures across your whole organisation.

Advanced Analytics Pipelines

Need custom KPI dashboards, automated data delivery to coaching staff, or a reporting layer built around your specific game model? We design the analytical infrastructure behind the decisions your club needs to make — calibrated to your methodology.

Have something in mind?

Tell us what you need. We'll scope it, give you a development fee, and build it directly into your platform. Most custom work is delivered within 2–4 weeks depending on complexity. No vague estimates — you'll know exactly what you're getting and what it costs before any work begins.
