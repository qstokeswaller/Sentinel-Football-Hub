What We Analyse

The detail coaches
don't have time for.

Match analysis takes hours. Most coaches review a squad of 20+. That means your individual performance rarely gets the attention it deserves. We fix that.

Movement & Positioning

Runs off the ball, positioning between lines, defensive shape, press triggers, and how you use space before receiving.

Technical Actions

Pass accuracy, first touch, ball retention under pressure, aerial duels, shooting technique, and 1v1 outcomes.

Decision Making

When to pass, when to drive, when to press — we track the quality of your decisions, not just the execution.

Defensive Contribution

Tackles won, interceptions, cover shadow, press intensity, defensive transitions, and positioning without the ball.

Statistical Summary

Key numbers for the match: touches, duels, key passes, goal involvement, and distance covered estimates where available.

Strengths & Development Areas

An honest, constructive summary of what's working and exactly where targeted training will make the biggest difference.

Professional Dossiers & Reports

Every report is formatted to a professional standard as a structured PDF dossier — suitable for sharing with coaches, scouts, or agents. Not a voice note, not a WhatsApp paragraph.

72-Hour Turnaround

Standard match reports are delivered within 72 hours of footage receipt. Full dossiers covering multiple matches may take up to 5 working days depending on volume.
